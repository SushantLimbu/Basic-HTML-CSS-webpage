About Us

Our Story

At Taco Fiesta, our story is one of love for authentic Mexican flavors and a deep-rooted commitment to quality. Founded by Alejandro Miguel in 1990, our restaurant was born out of a desire to bring the rich tapestry of Mexican culinary heritage to the community. Drawing inspiration from the bustling taquerias of Mexico City to the street vendors of Oaxaca, we've curated a menu that celebrates the diverse and colorful world of Mexican street food.

The Taco Eats Experience

Step into our cozy and lively atmosphere, where the aroma of sizzling meats, freshly made tortillas, and vibrant salsas fills the air. Whether you're joining us for a quick bite during your lunch break or a leisurely dinner with friends and family, we invite you to savor every moment at Taco Fiesta.
